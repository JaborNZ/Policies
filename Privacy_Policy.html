Information Collection and Purpose We collect information only when it is necessary to provide our services, maintain security, or improve the user experience. Marketing and Communications Service Communications: We will send you essential emails regarding your account, such as password resets, security alerts, or major service updates. Cookies and Tracking We use cookies and similar technologies to remember your preferences and improve performance. Essential Cookies: These are strictly necessary to provide you with services available through our App (e.g. keeping you logged in). Non-Essential Cookies: We use these for analytics and to understand how our services are being used. These are optional, and you can manage your consent for relevant cookies within the App's settings. 4. Third-Party Service Providers To ensure the successful delivery of our services, we partner with trusted third-party providers (e.g. cloud hosting, payment processors, or crash reporting tools). Compliance: We ensure our partners adhere to data protection standards that align with the New Zealand Privacy Act 2020. 5. Data Safety and Retention In accordance with Google Play's Data Safety standards: Encryption: All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (e.g. TLS).
